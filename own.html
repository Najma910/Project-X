ABOUT US



Why a website on women?


Women’s Stories is an online community run by Najma and Winnie to bring to the forefront inspirational women’s stories. We dedicate our website to the powerful and modern revolutionary women in history who have achieved momentous success in their career fields and subsequently on the world. In true intersectional feminist style, our website shows the stories of various successful women from all walks of life regardless of race, sexual orientation, religion, class background and geography.


What's next?


In displaying content of powerful women’s stories, we intend to use our website as part of an initiative to inspire young women of today and tomorrow that replicating the success of the women before us is possible. We want young girls and women to understand that they are the future of society and every girl and woman is capable to achieving greatness. As an online community, we welcome the work and voices of emerging feminists by providing a platform where anyone – from professionals to young girls – can contribute to this platform through the Join the Community page.

This website is constructed as part of the Code: First Girls Course Competition.
